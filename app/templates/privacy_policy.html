Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Daily Manna Email application ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service for receiving daily devotional content via email.

Information We Collect

Email Data

  • Recipient Email Addresses: Email addresses provided for receiving daily devotional emails
  • Sender Email Configuration: Gmail account credentials and settings for sending emails
  • Email Content: Daily devotional content fetched from external sources

Schedule Management Data

  • Content Schedule: Dates and selectors for organizing devotional content delivery
  • Delivery Status: Tracking of email send status, delivery dates, and notes
  • Administrative Notes: Internal notes for content management

Authentication Data

  • Dashboard Credentials: Username and password for accessing the administrative dashboard
  • OAuth Tokens: Google OAuth tokens for Gmail API access

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purpose

We use the collected information exclusively for:

  • Email Delivery: Sending automated daily devotional emails to subscribed recipients
  • Content Management: Organizing and scheduling devotional content delivery
  • Service Administration: Managing the email delivery system and troubleshooting issues

Data Processing

  • Content Fetching: Retrieving devotional content from authorized external sources (ezoe.work, churchintamsui.wixsite.com)
  • Email Processing: Formatting and sending emails via Gmail API
  • Schedule Processing: Managing delivery schedules and tracking send status
  • Reply Processing: Reading administrative email replies for schedule updates

Data Storage and Security

Storage Location

All data is stored on secure servers. Schedule data is stored locally in JSON files, while email processing uses Google's secure infrastructure.

Security Measures

  • Access Control: Administrative access requires authentication
  • OAuth Security: Google OAuth 2.0 for secure API access
  • Data Encryption: TLS encryption for email transmission
  • Minimal Permissions: OAuth scopes limited to required Gmail operations

Data Retention

Email addresses and schedule data are retained as long as the service is active. OAuth tokens are refreshed automatically and stored securely.

Third-Party Services

Google Services

This application integrates with Google Gmail for email sending and receiving. Google's Privacy Policy applies to data processed through their services.

Content Sources

Devotional content is sourced from external websites. We only fetch publicly available content and do not store personal data from these sources.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except as described in this policy. Email addresses are only used for sending the devotional content you requested.

Your Rights

  • Access: Administrative users can access stored data through the dashboard
  • Correction: Schedule data can be updated through the administrative interface
  • Deletion: Data can be removed upon request

International Data Transfers

Data may be processed and stored in different countries depending on server location and Google service usage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Information

For questions about this Privacy Policy or data handling practices, please contact the service administrators.
